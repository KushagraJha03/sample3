🌍 My Trajectory

My Journey

I was born in Cuttack, Odisha, India and spent my first 7 years in a small industrial township Joda in Odisha. With my parents, I then moved to Kolkata in West Bengal (formerly Bihar) in 1989. I moved to Delhi at age 19 and settled there for next 20 years. In Delhi, I completed my Schooling, Bachelors, Masters and PhD in May 2008.

I moved to Leipzig, Germany for my Postdoctoral work in March 2010. I shifted my base to Chile, then kept shuttling between Pisa and Leipzig till February 2015. I moved to Seattle, USA in March 2015, then to Trieste, Italy to continue my research. I returned to India in March 2016 to establish my research group at The Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai.
